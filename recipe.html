How to Make A Taco Rice Bowl

Ingredients (serves 2)

  • 2 bowls of rice
  • 200g minced meat
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 avocado
  • 4 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons tonkatsu sauce
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Mince onions, shred lettuce, and dice tomato and avocado
  2. Mix together ketchup, tonkatsu sauce, and curry powder in a small bowl
  3. Head oil in a pan over medium heat
  4. Cook minced meat and onions in pan, and add sauce once the meat is cooked
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Serve over rice, and top with lettuce, tomatoes and avocadoes
